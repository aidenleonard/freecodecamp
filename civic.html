honda civic 2006 EX Coupe: The Sexiest Car Ever

Engine

The honda civic ex coupe features a 1.8L r18 naturally aspirated VTEC engine. This engine outputs an impressive 140hp stock, giving it a nasty 78hp/L. This vicious amount of power sends the car flying with a tap of the accelerator. Just plain nasty.

Transmission

The transmission on this bad boy is a 5 speed manual with a custom short shifter. The stock shifter geometry was modified using a small rubber arm extender which reduced the throw of the shift knob itself. This resulted in a significant reduction in shift throw, and subjectively instantaneous shifts. I often exclaim: "Oh baby" whenever I have the pleasure of shifting from 2nd to 3rd gear.
